About El Rokh

Captain Mohamed El Rokh is a certified fitness trainer affiliated with the Egyptian and African Bodybuilding Federations. With his expertise, he delivers effective training programs tailored to individual needs, both in-person and online. Known for his dedication, he motivates clients to achieve their fitness goals. He also owns "Muscles State EG" gyms, providing a conducive environment for effective workouts.
